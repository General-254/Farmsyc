FarmSyncBot

A free and open-source digital assistant for farmers in Kenya. Built by Jephthah Muchangi.

What It Can Do:

  • ✔️ Daily farm reminders
  • ✔️ Expense tracking
  • ✔️ Market prices & alerts
  • ✔️ Weather updates
  • ✔️ Voice/text farm reports
  • ✔️ Pest/disease guidance
  • ✔️ Training resources
