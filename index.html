FarmSyncBot

A free & open-source digital assistant for farmers in Kenya, built by Jephthah Muchangi.

What It Does

  • Daily reminders (watering, feeding, harvesting)
  • Expense & income tracking
  • Market price updates
  • Weather alerts
  • Farm reports (text, voice)
  • Pest & disease tips
  • Training & resources
