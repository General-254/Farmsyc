Revolutionize Your Farming Routine

FarmSyncBot empowers farmers with real-time reports, automated reminders, smart coordination tools and more — all from your phone.

1. Daily Farm Reports

Track crop health, livestock stats, and input usage automatically, with data summaries sent to your WhatsApp or Webex daily.

2. Task Reminders

Receive alerts for weeding, spraying, feeding, harvesting and more—never miss a critical farm task again.

3. Group Coordination

Plan and assign farm tasks within your team or cooperative using smart bots in WhatsApp and Webex spaces.

4. Disease & Weather Alerts

Stay ahead of weather risks and disease outbreaks with AI-powered notifications and advice customized for your region.

5. Inventory & Budget Logs

Keep digital records of what you buy, use, and sell on the farm. Simple and accessible from anywhere.

6. Automated Reports

Instantly generate PDF reports for inspections, certifications or internal review with one tap.

7. Free and Open-Source

FarmSyncBot is 100% free. Open-source and customizable, so even smallholder farmers can benefit without paying a shilling.
